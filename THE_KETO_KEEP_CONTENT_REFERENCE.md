## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is The Keto Keep?
The Keto Keep is a supportive online community led by National Board Certified Health & Wellness Coaches, created for anyone practicing low-carb lifestyles like keto, paleo, carnivore, or other ketogenic approaches. We provide guidance, education, and connection to help you thrive.

### Who is this community for?
Our space is for anyone following or exploring ketogenic diets—whether you're brand new, experienced, or anywhere in between. If you value a compassionate, evidence-informed, and collaborative environment, you'll feel right at home.

### What do I get when I join?
Membership includes access to: Professional health coach support, a welcoming like-minded community, resources on nutrition, sleep, stress, movement, light, and relationships, educational livestreams and Q&As, and opportunities to work privately with our coaches if desired.

### Is this a replacement for medical advice?
No. While our coaches are trained professionals, The Keto Keep is not a substitute for medical care. We encourage members to maintain regular communication with their healthcare providers for medical concerns.

### Do I have to follow a strict version of keto to join?
Not at all! Whether you practice paleo, low-carb, keto, carnivore, or another ketogenic variation, you're welcome here. Our community celebrates the diversity of approaches under the ketogenic umbrella.

### What makes The Keto Keep different from other groups?
Unlike typical diet forums or social media groups, The Keto Keep is: Coach-led (run by board-certified health coaches), science-based and compassionate, holistic (covering nutrition, sleep, stress, lifestyle, and relationships), and collaborative (focusing on encouragement, not judgment).

### Can I work one-on-one with a coach?
Yes. If you'd like personalized support, you can choose to work privately with any of our hosting coaches through their individual practices.

### How do the livestreams work?
We host regular live educational sessions and Q&As where you can learn, ask questions, and interact with coaches and fellow members. Replays are usually available if you can't join live.

### Is The Keto Keep only online?
Yes, currently The Keto Keep is an online community, making it accessible to members across the country (and beyond).

### How do I get started?
Simply sign up, introduce yourself, and jump into the conversation! Our coaches and members are ready to welcome you and help you make the most of your low-carb journey.
